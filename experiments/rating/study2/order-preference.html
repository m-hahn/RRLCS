Instructions

You will read a series of English sentences.

For each sentence, you will be asked how hard it is to understand.

Read each sentence only once, and make your rating on the basis of your first impression.

After some of the sentences, you will be asked a comprehension question.

Some of the sentences contain attention checks. We will alert you if you fail an attention check. Don't worry if that happens a few times.

Example

Please read the following sentence:


The librarian put the book onto the shelf.


In the experiment, you will be asked to rate: How hard is this sentence to understand?

Easy to understand 1 2 3 4 5 Hard to understand


This sentence is simple and easy to understand. `1' would be an appropriate score. Please select `1' and press `Continue'.

Please provide a correct answer before continuing.
